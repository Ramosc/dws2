Crystal Lopez

Hello Dolls & Gents Welcome to my site where like many makeup is a passion not a hobby so i am glad to introduce myself and a little more about this site. Let me start by saying my name is Crystal, I am a freelance makeup artist in the central florida area. My specialty is of course makeup and anything fashion related. I started this site in the hope that soon i can become a professional makeup artist and share my art work with the world. Many people think women use makeup to hide ugliness or cover something up but i don't believe this for a second my philosophy is makeup is an art and a way to show the best side of you. Although, makeup can hide many imperfections it can also unveil the true beauty from within. With that being said I want to welcome you to my page. Ladies and gentleman if you are in the central Florida location and are looking for the right look for an event or even just want to look your best contact me below. I am gladly accepting all bookings now. Feel free to join my Facebook page as well. I hope to hear from you soon and may your days be filled with the Lords blessings.
