Crystal Lopez

Hello Dolls & Gents welcome to my site where beauty resides. Makeup is a passion not just a hobby so I am glad to introduce myself. Let me start by saying my name is Crystal, I am a freelance makeup artist in the central florida area. My specialty is of course makeup and anything fashion related. I became a professional makeup artist and wanted to share my art work with the world. Many people think women uses makeup to hide ugliness or cover something up, but i don't believe this for a second my philosophy is makeup is an art and a way to show the best side of you. Although, makeup can hide many imperfections it can also unveil the true beauty from within. With that being said if you are in the central Florida area and are looking for the right look for an event or even just want to look your best for a night out on the town i'm here to make you stunning. I am gladly accepting all bookings now. Please like us on Facebook and I hope to hear from you soon; may your days be filled with the Lords blessings.
